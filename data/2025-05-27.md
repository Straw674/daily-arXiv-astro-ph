Background: 磁化环形坍缩是恒星形成早期阶段的自然表现，连接了分子云核坍缩的前恒星和原恒星阶段。现有研究在磁场扩散性和分辨率方面存在不足。

Data: 使用了非理想磁流体动力学（Ohmic diffusivity η）和高分辨率模拟数据。

Method: 采用了能够覆盖更广磁化参数H0范围的代码，并进行了非理想磁流体动力学模拟。

Result: 研究发现伪盘在所有H0和η值下均形成，中心质量增长率与先前结果一致；磁场扩散性影响了伪盘结构和速度比，并可能引发丰富的天体化学过程。

Abstract: The collapse of singular magnetized toroids (Li & Shu 1996) is a natural
representation of an early phase in star formation, bridging the prestellar and
protostellar phases of the collapse of molecular cloud cores. We revisit the
collapse study of Allen et al. (2003b), now with explicit nonideal MHD (Ohmic
diffusivity $\eta$) and higher resolution using a code able to cover a broader
range of the magnetization parameter $H_0$. Galli-Shu equatorial pseudodisks
form for all values of $H_0$ and $\eta$, and the asymptotic central mass growth
rate is in the scale $\dot{M}_*\sim(a^3/G)(1+H_0)$, where $a$ is the isothermal
sound speed, consistent with previous results and predictions. The explicit
Ohmic diffusivity makes the field line structure less radial than in previous
work, connecting the pseudodisk more effectively to its surroundings. Matter
can fall efficiently onto the pseudodisk surfaces, forming oblique shocks,
where shock heating and large density gradients raise the possibility of rich
astrochemistry. Pseudodisk size and structure are influenced by magnetic
diffusivity. Force and velocity ratios were computed to explore the magnetic
support within the pseudodisk and its induced slowdown in infall velocity.
Magnetic diffusivity was measured to control the strength of these effects and
their location within the pseudodisk. The dependence of the field line
configurations, pseudodisk structure, and velocity ratios on magnetic
diffusivity has observable consequences for collapsing envelopes.
