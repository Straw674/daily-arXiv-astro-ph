Data: 研究使用了致密分子云的电离数据，并讨论了未来可能通过银河系中心附近和银道面上方的分子云样本、宇宙射线电离贡献的详细建模以及尘埃消光图对气体密度的精细分析来改进数据。

Method: 通过分析分子云的电离现象，结合暗物质模型的理论框架，设置了暗物质模型的约束条件。

Result: 研究结果表明，分子云的电离现象是探测亚keV暗物质的最有力工具之一，并对多种暗物质模型设置了强约束。

Abstract: We show that the ionization of dense molecular clouds can be used to set
strong constraints on dark matter models producing UV/X-ray photons in their
annihilation or decay. We place robust and competitive constraints on various
dark matter models, such as axion-like particles, scalars and sterile
neutrinos, for masses between $\sim30$~eV and $10$~keV, and project forecasts
to illustrate the potential of this target. We discuss how these constraints
can be significantly improved by considering a more refined sample of molecular
clouds near the Galactic Center and above the Galactic plane, a detailed
modeling of the cosmic-ray ionization contribution and, potentially, a more
refined analysis of the gas density in clouds through dust extinction maps.
Thus, ionization of molecular clouds emerges as one of the most powerful tools
for probing sub-keV dark matter.
