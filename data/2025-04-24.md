Abstract: Imaging reconstruction of interferometric data is a hard ill-posed inverse
problem. Its difficulty is increased when observing the Galactic Center, which

Galactic Center. In this work we present a novel strategy to mitigate its

horizon scale works on the Galactic Center at 86G Hz up to 116 GHz, and the
