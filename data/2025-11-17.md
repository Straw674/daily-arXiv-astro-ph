**TL;DR**: 这篇论文通过考虑前身星坍缩前捕获的暗物质（DM）对暗光子（dark photon）自由程和超新星冷却速率的影响，发现不对称暗物质（asymmetric DM）可累积形成“暗光球层（dark photosphere）”抑制暗光子辐射，从而重新开放了此前被排除的参数空间，强调了在推导暗区（dark sectors）恒星冷却限制时考虑天体物理暗物质分布的重要性。

Background: 研究聚焦于利用核坍缩超新星（core-collapse supernovae）探索暗光子（dark photons）等弱耦合粒子，并探讨了前身星坍缩前捕获的暗物质（DM）对暗光子自由程及超新星冷却效率的影响。

Data: 研究中使用的数据来源包括超新星SN1987A的中微子观测数据（observed neutrino emission）以及暗物质（DM）在恒星前身星（progenitor star）内捕获效应的模拟数据（simulation data）。

Method: 采用自洽分析方法，结合轻媒介效应计算暗物质（DM）捕获率，并分析湮灭型和非对称型暗物质对暗光子自由程及超新星冷却速率的影响。

Result: result：不对称暗物质（asymmetric DM）可累积形成“暗光球层”（dark photosphere），抑制暗光子（dark photon）光度并重新开放参数空间中被排除的区域。

Abstract: Core-collapse supernovae serve as powerful probes of light, weakly coupled particles, such as dark photons. The conventional SN1987A cooling bound constrains the dark photon mass-mixing parameter space by requiring that the luminosity from the proto-neutron star core not exceed the observed neutrino emission. In this work, we revisit these limits by including the effect of dark matter (DM) captured inside the progenitor star before collapse. The trapped DM acts as an additional scattering target for dark photons, modifying their free-streaming length and, consequently, the supernova cooling rate. We perform a self-consistent analysis for both annihilating and asymmetric DM scenarios, incorporating light-mediator effects in the capture rate calculation. For annihilating DM, the equilibrium density remains too small to affect the bounds significantly. In contrast, asymmetric DM can accumulate to large densities, leading to the formation of a "dark photosphere" that suppresses the dark-photon luminosity and reopens previously excluded regions of parameter space. Our results emphasise the importance of accounting for astrophysical DM populations when deriving stellar-cooling constraints on dark sectors.
